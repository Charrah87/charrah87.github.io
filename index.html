About Me

Built from the ground up.

I'm a Senior Product Manager specializing in enterprise API platforms, backend data systems, and high-throughput infrastructure. My career arc is unusual — I didn't start in product. My edge is operational clarity. I eliminate friction between ideas and execution — turning chaotic backlogs into high-velocity teams, and high-risk migrations into non-events.
